Why this matters
Misinformation in India
⚠
Over 500 million WhatsApp users in India make it the world's largest vector for viral misinformation. Health hoaxes, political deepfakes, and financial scams spread faster than corrections.
Emotional triggers
Outrage and urgency make people share before thinking. TruthLens flags these patterns automatically.
Out-of-context media
Real images reused with false captions. Our AI checks metadata and cross-references known databases.
Fake authority
Fabricated quotes from doctors and officials. TruthLens verifies claimed sources against trusted records.
